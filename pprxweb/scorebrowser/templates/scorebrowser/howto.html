Welcome to PPR X, where you can guide your DDR improvement journey with LIFE4 requirements tracking, and data-informed improvement targets.

To use PPR X you'll need an existing account with 3icecream, connected to a Konami ID. Once you've got that, register for a PPR X account. Confirm your email to activate your account. After logging in, you'll be asked to link your 3icecream account, which allows PPR X to fetch your scores from there. Then, you'll be able to see your scores on PPR X.

This site provides two main features:

  1. It checks your progress against each rank requirement of each LIFE4 rank. It provides you a clean way to see when you have achieved what you need for each rank, and lets you easily pick out which charts to try to improve to meet those requirements.
  2. It assigns most scores (including all expert and most challenge charts) a "quality" rating, normalized to the chart's difficulty (its "spice" rating), providing you with information about your strong and weak charts.

If you have any questions, head on over to the LIFE4 Discord server and speak up in #pprx-help!
